About Us

At Gorkhali Coffee, we are passionate about bringing the authentic taste of Nepal to coffee lovers everywhere.
Our café is dedicated to sourcing the finest beans from local farmers, ensuring a sustainable and high-quality coffee experience.
Our skilled baristas are trained to craft each cup with love, precision, and the rich traditions of Nepali coffee culture.

Join us in our cozy space, where every sip of coffee tells a story, and every visit feels like home.
